The toasts can be customized to six basic styles, toast-primary (primary color),toast-success (green), toast-warning (yellow), toast-info (blue) toast-error (red) and toast-secondary(secondary color). 

            
                <div class="toast">
                    <div class="toast-header toast-success">
                        <strong class="push-to-right">My Simple Toast</strong>
                        <small>5 mins ago</small>
                        <button type="button" class="btn-close ms-2 mb-1">
                            <span aria-hidden="true"></span>
                        </button>
                    </div>
                    <div class="toast-body">
                        Hello, world! This is a toast message.
                    </div>
                </div>
