The Solid Cards can also be customized to five styles, bg-primary (primary color),bg-success (green), bg-warning (yellow), bg-info (blue) bg-error (red). 

            
                <div class="card bg-primary mb-3" style="max-width: 25rem;">
                    <div class="card-header">Header</div>
                    <div class="card-body bg-primary">
                        <h4 class="card-title">Primary card title</h4>
                        <p class="card-text">Some quick example text to build on the card title and make up the bulk of the
                            card's content.</p>
                    </div>
                </div>

The Outlined Cards can be customized to five styles, border-primary (primary color),border-success (green), border-warning (yellow), border-info (blue) border-error (red). 

            
                <div class="card border-primary mb-3" style="max-width: 25rem;">
                    <div class="card-header">Header</div>
                    <div class="card-body ">
                        <h4 class="card-title">Primary card title</h4>
                        <p class="card-text">Some quick example text to build on the card title and make up the bulk of the
                            card's content.</p>
                    </div>
                </div>

The width of the Cards can further be played around with, style="max-width: 20rem;" part of the component script.
