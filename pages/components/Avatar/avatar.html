Standard Avatars


The avatar can be customized to five styles, avatar-xl (12em), avatar-lg (10em), avatar-std (8em) avatar-sm (6em), and avatar-xs (4em). 

            
            <div class=" avatar-xl">
                <img src="..." alt="..." class="avatar"/>
            </div>

            <div class=" avatar-std">
                <img src="..." alt="..." class="avatar"/>
            </div>

Avatars with activity status

Avatars support presence indicators. There are three indicators Online, Offline and Away.


        <div class=" avatar-std">
        <img src="..." alt="..." class="avatar online"/>
        </div>

        <div class=" avatar-std">
        <img src="..." alt="..." class="avatar away"/>
        </div>
